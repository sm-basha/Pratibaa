Empowering Intelligences With Multiple Intelligence Theory

We use Multiple Intelligence Theory to help the students to easily blend into a multi-faceted world we live in and the curriculum is planned in such a way that it enables every child to sharpen his/her respective intelligence.

Elements Of Multiple Intelligence Theory: 

We use Multiple Intelligence Theory to help the students to easily blend into a multi-faceted world we live in and the curriculum is planned in such a way that it enables every child to sharpen his/her respective intelligence.

Linguistic Intelligence: Improving communication skills through creative writing, poetry, public speaking, debates and a lot more.

Musical Intelligence: Recognizing tonal patterns, remembering melodies, understanding the structure and rhythm of both classical and contemporary music.

Bodily-Kinesthetic Intelligence: Controlling the automatic and voluntary movement and progresses by using the body in highly differentiated ways.

Visual-Spatial Intelligence: Expanding creativity through visual arts like photography, imagination, sketching, painting etc.

Logical-Mathematical Intelligence: Enhancing critical thinking skills through problem-solving, performing complex mathematical calculations etc.

Intrapersonal Intelligence: Self-reflection of one’s strengths and weaknesses.

Interpersonal intelligence: Developing social intelligence through group dynamics, establishing positive relations with other people etc

Naturalistic intelligence: Exploring one’s interdependent relationship with flora and fauna through camping, nature walk etc.

Professional Development Program for Glendale Teachers

To foster a healthy culture of learning within the classroom and to generate effective interaction with students, teachers at Glendale uses creativity, humor, and imagination. This is done to develop schemes of work and to plan lessons.

The teaching skills of our teachers are groomed and refined through a Professional Development Program. This program aims to make the teachers well aware of the most effective and innovative trends of teaching. Every week they have a period, especially for professional development. During the regular tea-time talks, teachers exchange their professional development class' learning experience with each other. Also, there are external resource persons to help teachers learn cutting-edge teaching techniques.

ABOUT GLENDALE EDUCATION

Glendale is known for its academic excellence and state-of-the-art pedagogy. At Glendale, we follow a unique philosophy of internationally researched methodologies that integrates academics, personality development and universal human values for the holistic growth of our students. Our teachers are committed and dynamic. They are trained in-house to bring out the best in each student.

Glendale has three remarkable campuses in three thriving locations across Hyderabad.

GLENDALE ACADEMY:

Glendale Academy is the main branch situated in Sun City. It was established in 2005, and it has both, a Central Board of Secondary Education (CBSE) and a Cambridge curriculum. It also prepares students for the All India Secondary School Examination (AISSE) administered by the CBSE on completion of Class X & XII.

Glendale Academy is spread over ten-acre campus that offers state-of-the-art facilities like, and a lot more. We offer both CBSE & Cambridge curricula that prepare students to become global citizens.

 It also has EdSense, which is a comprehensive education management platform for the school, which ensures higher student achievement and teacher effectiveness.

GLENDALE INTERNATIONAL SCHOOL (IBPYP)

Glendale International School IB PYP, estd. 2015 is a full-time day school which caters classes from Pre-Primary to 7th grade. It is situated in Tellapur with a campus spread across 1.5 acre of land area.

It is a nurturing place where young artists explore and find their medium and blossom. The PYP (Primary Years Program) helps in creating a curriculum that is more engaging, relevant, challenging and significant for learners in the 3-12 age range.

At our school, Academics and Arts share the centre stage. Fostering the 21st-century skills of creativity, communication, collaboration, critical thinking and character, we work on our mission of preparing global citizens. In Glendale International School IB PYP, the talent of a student is tapped and fostered by creating opportunities for it to flourish to its full potential.

GLENDALE INTERNATIONAL SCHOOL (Cambridge)

Our brand new Glendale campus, Glendale International School, is spread across 3 acres of land.
